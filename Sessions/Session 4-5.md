#session
**“The Goblin Hunts”**

Quest Giver: [[Harbin Wester]]
- After a council meeting in Phandalin, Harbin hires the party to deal with multiple goblin bands operating in the nearby mountains.
- He offers payment (partly backed by Stonehaven’s Adventuring Guild) and “official hero recognition.”
- The job includes clearing:
    - A forest camp - goblins ambushing travellers.
    - A mountain outlook - occupied by a goblin hunters looking to ambush travellers going between Phandalin and Stonehaven
- These sessions are open-ended, the party can choose which camp to go to first.

Chauntea is the goddess most commonly worshipped in rural communities. She represents agriculture, growth, and the continued survival of ordinary people through hard work and patience.

Her worship is practical rather than grand. Farmers pray to her for good harvests, townsfolk for stability, and travelers for safe passage through settled lands. Shrines to Chauntea are often simple and maintained by the community rather than clergy.
